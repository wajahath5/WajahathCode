Job Description:

L3Harris Agile Development Engineering (ADE) group is seeking Scrum Master who will act as a servant leader to lead Agile practices and promoting and supporting Scrum teams to develop customer-centric digital software applications and services.  We are looking for someone who enjoys working in a highly collaborative environment that values team collaboration, understand and practices Scrum theory, values in quality and has a strong desire to inspire change as we continue on our agile transformational journey.

Essential Functions:

Facilitate Scrum events (Daily scrum, Sprint Planning, Review/Demo and Retrospective) as requested or needed
Work alongside Product Owners to refine and groom the product backlog, forecast sprint iterations, and resolve team/stakeholder conflicts
Coaching the Scrum Team in self-organization and cross-functionality
Anticipate and addresses potential or actual impediments to team delivery
Coach the Scrum Team to release Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
Track progress through backlog tracking, burn down metrics, velocity, etc.
Coach teams, Product Owners, and Managers to ensure on-time, and on-budget, quality delivery
Understand and practice agility throughout the Product Life Cycle
Coach and encourage continual improvement and innovation in the platform and application development process
Work with other Scrum Masters to increase the effectiveness of the application of Scrum in the organization
Coach the Scrum Team in organizational environments in which Scrum is not yet fully adopted and understood
Support the Product Owner’s vision, goals, scope, and product domain
Lead and Coach employees and stakeholders to understand, enact, and adopt Scrum practices
Champion change that increases the productivity of the Scrum Team
Adopt a positive attitude about Agile processes and facilitate the basics of the mindset and process (i.e.transparency)
Support Agile transformation process including PI planning, templates, artifacts, training materials, and lessons-learned
Qualifications:

Able to obtain and maintain a Department of Defense Security Clearance, which requires U.S. Citizenship.
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Administration or related discipline or equivalent experience.
3 to 5 years of leading multiple Scrum teams is required; preferably within the Defense and Aerospace industries, but not required.
SAFe® certifications (e.g. SSM, SASM, ASE etc.) and/or Scrum Master certification  (e.g. CSM or PSM) are required
Experience with Agile tools (e.g. Jira, Rally, Confluence, EasyBI, AIO etc.) is required
Must be able to communicate effectively and work in a team environment is required
Preferred Additional Skills:

Master's in any Engineering discipline, Master of Science in Management program or Business Administration
Experience implementing best Agile practices and standards, and mentoring/teaching junior practitioners
Direct, hands-on experience with writing user stories, grooming product backlogs, and coordinating and prioritizing conflicting requirements in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment
